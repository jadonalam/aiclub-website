Welcome to the AI Club

The AI Club at Pleasant Grove High School is a dynamic, student-run organization dedicated to exploring the exciting world of Artificial Intelligence (AI). Established in 2023, our club has been at the forefront of fostering a deep understanding of AI among students.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create an engaging and informative environment where students can learn about AI technologies, develop their skills, and apply their knowledge in practical projects. We aim to inspire the next generation of innovators and thinkers who will shape the future of AI.

What We Do

Our activities include regular meetings, workshops, guest lectures by AI professionals, and participation in AI-related competitions. We also collaborate on projects that give students hands-on experience in areas like machine learning, robotics, and data analysis.

Our Achievements

Over the years, our club has achieved remarkable milestones. Our members have won awards in various AI competitions and have successfully organized school-wide AI awareness events. These achievements reflect our commitment to excellence and innovation in the field of AI.

Join Us

If you're a student at Pleasant Grove High School and are curious about AI, we welcome you to join our club! It's a great opportunity to learn, collaborate, and grow in this fascinating field. We meet 1st and 3rd Tuesdays of every month in HM9 (Mr. Reed's Classroom). See you there!
